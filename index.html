HEAL

Helping Teens Become Global Health Citizens

Who are we?

We are a teen-based non-profit organization that aims to educate teens on health issues while also giving them the tools to become global health citizens and make a difference worldwide one country at a time.

How do we do this?

Every 3 months (1 quarter of the year) we pick a new health topic to focus on. We publish articles educating about these health conditions and include other organizations that are focused on fundraising for or treating these health issues. Every quarter we share a personal story from someone who has lived with this health issue along with lots of educational content so you can learn and understand our focus well. For every health focus we have, we also have a country that is affected by it and is our chosen country to aid. We plan fundraisers and events to help people in our chosen country get treated or to raise funds for research and development.

How can I help?

You can become a part of our community by joining us in our mission to become global health citizens. All you have to do is read and learn about the health focus of the quarter, and choose how you would like to support our goal. By educating yourself, you are becoming a knowledgeable citizen and are already halfway to making a difference worldwide. Once you’ve learned about our focus and browsed through our linked content, you’re ready to take action! This is where you help us raise awareness for our health issue and collect donations for an organization in another country in order to make a difference. Or try one of our suggested global and local activities. Now you’re all set to become a global health citizen and make an impact worldwide, ready… set… GO!
